## Description
A Stacked Pyramid is used when two or more data sets are displayed on top of each other and grouped together under the same categories. A population pyramid, also called an age pyramid or age picture diagram, is a graphical illustration that shows the distribution of various age groups in a population, which forms the shape of a pyramid when the population is growing.
