## Description
A Dotplot or Dumbbell Chart is used to display a difference. It is useful for showing changes between two points in time, two conditions, or between two groups. At each end, the line has its attributed colour dot (2 colours in total, one for each category being represented).

## Points of attention
- When showing two or more values per category, connect the dots with a horizontal line. The gap length encodes the difference and makes comparisons much easier than reading two separate positions.
