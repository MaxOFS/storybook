## Description
A Dotplot or Dumbbell Chart is used to display a difference. It is useful for showing changes between two points in time, two conditions, or between two groups. At each end, the line has its attributed color dot (2 colors in total, one for each category being represented).

## Do's and don'ts
- When showing two or more values per category, connect the dots with a horizontal line. The gap length encodes the difference and makes comparisons much easier than reading two separate positions.
