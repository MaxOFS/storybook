# Corporate design
The corporate design define the FSO-specific elements relative to data visualisation : the color palettes derived from the Confederation's identity, the typographic and layout conventions shared across our publications, the standard UI elements used in interactive products, and the mandatory texts such as source references, copyright notices, and methodological footnotes that frame our statistical outputs.

This part of the guidelines is used in order to guarantee an uniform and standard way of deciphering the products being produced and published by the FSO. Consistency across output types and publication channels reinforces the credibility and visibility that our data deserves.

This corporate design is based on elements taken from the Confederation's design system (CD Bund). Where our office has developped some specific conventions to adress some specific needs, they are also documented in this chapter.
