# Accessibility
All of the Confederation’s Internet resources must be designed in such a way as to enable people with disabilities to use them without restrictions.

Accessibility has a clear legal framework, starting with a reference in the Federal Constitution (Art. 8, paragraph 2): 

“ No person may be discriminated […] because of a physical, mental or psychological disability. ”

The eCH0059 standard promotes the use of information and services offered on websites and mobile applications and ensures access to them regardless of existing restrictions or disabilities.

The Federal Administration is committed to fulfilling the requirements of the “ Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1) ” to conformance level AA.
